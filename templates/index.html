Syllabus

Chapter 1: Personal Finance Foundation

Module 1: Money Management and Budgeting

• Introduction to money and its importance
• Basics of budgeting and differentiating needs from wants
• Tips for avoiding debt and recognizing predatory lending practices
• Responsible use of credit cards

Module 2: Banking, Savings, and Emergency Fund

• Basics of banking services and opening a bank account
• Importance of saving and setting savings goals
• Setting up and maintaining an emergency fund
• Examples of emergency situations and how to financially prepare for them

Module 3: Credit, Debt Management, and Financial Goals

• Introduction to credit cards and responsible usage
• Understanding loans, repayment strategies, and avoiding debt traps
• Setting and adjusting short- and long-term financial goals with a plan for achievement.

Module 4: Investment Basics and Risk Management

• Basics of investing and different investment options
• Understanding risks and benefits of investments
• Importance of risk management in financial decision-making

Module 5: Insurance and Financial Wellness

• Purpose and types of insurance
• Understanding premiums, coverage, and risk management
• Importance of financial health and achieving financial wellness
• Seeking professional help for financial guidance if needed

Chapter 2: Advanced Financial Concepts

Module 6: Real Estate, Taxes, and Financial Fraud Prevention

• Understanding real estate and the decision of renting vs buying
• Basics of property taxes and their importance
• Identifying financial frauds and scams and how to prevent them

Module 7: Financial Markets and Digital Financial Services

• Introduction to financial markets and their role
• Basics of the stock market and investment strategies
• Introduction to digital financial services and their benefits
• Using digital services safely and securely

Module 8: Global Financial Awareness and Practical Applications

• Understanding the international economy and its impact
• Importance of global financial literacy and staying informed
• Applying financial knowledge to real-life scenarios through problem-solving.

Module 9: Financial Wellness and Professional Help

• Importance of financial health and tips for achieving financial wellness
• Seeking professional help for financial guidance and assistance
• Developing strategies for maintaining financial well-being in the long term

Module 10: Review and Conclusion

• Reviewing key concepts covered in the course
• Reflecting on personal financial goals and achievements
• Planning for continued financial education and growth
